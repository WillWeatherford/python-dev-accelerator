Acquire a Heroku Account¶

Tasks¶

Go to www.heroku.com and sign up for an account (if you already have an account that’s great, no need to make another one).

After you’ve created your account, follow the Getting Started with Python on Heroku (Links to an external site.) tutorial to install the command-line tools you’ll need and set them up.

DO NOT follow through with step 3 of the tutorial, “Prepare the app”. You will do that tomorrow.

You will need this for your assignment Tuesday, so ensure that you complete this assignment on time.

Submitting Your Assignment¶

Take a screenshot of your heroku account homepage (the apps tab) and upload it.

Use the comment feature in canvas to submit any questions, reflections, or sources and collaborations you used to complete the assignment.
