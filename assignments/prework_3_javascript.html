Learn the Basics of JavaScript¶

During the 401 Python course, we will be learning a great deal of Python. But we will also be making use of technologies related to web development that are not Python. To work in web development, you’ll need to have a solid understanding of JavaScript and how it works in the client.

Tasks¶

Spend the time between now and the start of class sharpening your JavaScript skills.

If you are a complete beginner, then may I suggest an account with either Treehouse or CodeAcademy. Once you are signed up, either site has a number of quality tutorials that cover JavaScript. Pick one, and work through it. Then pick the next in line and keep going. Spend at least an hour a day working on this.

Submitting Your Work¶

There are no points for this assignment, nor is there anything to submit.
