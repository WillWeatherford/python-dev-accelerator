Group Project: Form Groups¶

It is now time to begin preparations for your Group Project. The next step for group projects is to form groups around an idea.

Tasks¶

Previously, you held a pitch session to generate ideas for group projects. Take time throughout the 24 hours following that session to discuss the options with each-other. Form groups around the ideas that you will implement. Consider carefully your groups. You will want a breadth of skill sets and interests. You will be working together closely, so make sure you feel comfortable with each-other.

Group Parameters

  • No group may consist of fewer than 3 members
  • No group may consist of more than 5 members

Submitting Your Work¶

Use the provided text area to write down the names of all the members of your team. Additionally, provide the name and summary of your selected idea.

Each team member must provide this submission.
